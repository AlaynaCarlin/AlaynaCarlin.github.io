About Me

Hello, I'm Alayna Carlin. At the age of sixteen I got a job at the Subway near my home and began my journey learning to serve people and work on a team. After about a year an a half I switched jobs to become a barista at the local coffee shop and acquire new experiences that would strengthen my skills as a team player.

After graduating high school in May of 2021 I enrolled in coding classes at Eleven Fifty Academy with the goal of learning Web Development. I have always been interested in code, and I was thrilled when the opportunity arose for me to attend classes at Eleven Fifty.

I am a motivated learner and hard worker, and I look forward to strengthening my skills and expanding my knowledge in a professional work environment.
